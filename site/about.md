# About this blog

Posts here start life as research notes produced by an agentic research pipeline,
get reviewed and approved in Notion, and are then drafted into concept-first
articles by a content writing agent. Every published post was reviewed by a human
before it went live.

The pipeline:

1. The research team generates notes and uploads them to a Notion database.
2. Notes are reviewed in Notion and marked "Ready for Blog" when approved.
3. A generator pulls approved notes, drafts a ~700 word concept-first post, and
   saves it as a Markdown draft.
4. The draft is reviewed, edited if needed, and published to this site.
